How Super Chat fees work

With Super Chats, tips, and donations, the streamer does not receive the full amount a viewer sends. The platform deducts a fee, and the remainder is the streamer's payout (take-home). This calculator uses a simple "amount × (100 − fee)%" model, and the fee rate is fully editable.

Why desktop and mobile apps differ

Purchases made inside mobile apps add App Store (Apple) or Google Play processing fees on top of the platform's own cut, so the same amount yields a smaller payout than via desktop/web. The preset values (YouTube web ≈ 30%, app ≈ 45%, and so on) are widely cited approximations that vary by payment route, contract, and time — always confirm the exact split in each platform's current terms.
