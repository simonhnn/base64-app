Privacy policy

How this site handles data.

Information collected

We store the conversion direction (encode/decode) and the non-Base64 side of the string (the plain-text side).

Purpose of use

Used to improve the service and understand usage trends. The conversion itself is processed in your browser.

Sharing with third parties

The conversion data we collect and store (the conversion direction and the plain-text side) is never provided or sold to third parties.

Advertising

To cover operating costs, this site uses the third-party ad service "A-ADS (Anonymous Ads)". Ads are served from a-ads.com in an iframe. A-ADS is a privacy-focused ad network that, as a rule, does not track users with cookies or collect personally identifiable information. However, when an ad loads, information necessary for the connection, such as your IP address and browser type, may be sent to the ad provider.
