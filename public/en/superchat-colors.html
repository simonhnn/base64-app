What the color tells you

The color is a quick signal of how much was sent. As the amount rises it goes blue → light blue → teal → yellow → orange → magenta → red, and both the message length and the time pinned at the top of chat increase.

What is a "red Super Chat"?

A red Super Chat is a high-value one shown in red, starting at $100. It stays pinned for 1 hour at $100–$199.99, and the pin time grows with the amount — up to 5 hours at $500 or more.
