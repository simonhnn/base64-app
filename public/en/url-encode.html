What is URL encoding?

URL encoding (percent-encoding) converts characters that are not allowed in a URL — spaces, non-ASCII text, and reserved symbols — into a % followed by hexadecimal digits. It is commonly used for query strings and form values. This tool performs encodeURIComponent / decodeURIComponent style conversion in your browser and never sends your input to a server.
