How the estimate works

This tool estimates ad revenue as "monthly views ÷ 1,000 × RPM". RPM (revenue per 1,000 views) varies widely by genre, country, and season, so the result is shown as a range (low–high). The defaults are rough guides — adjust them to match your experience.

Pasting a channel URL fetches public stats (subscribers, total views, estimated views over the last 30 days) to seed the estimate. Data is retrieved only through each platform's official API — no HTML scraping.

Important notes

  • Figures are estimates, not actual or confirmed earnings.
  • They cover ad revenue only — Super Chats, memberships, sponsorships, and merch are excluded.
  • This site is not affiliated with YouTube, Twitch, TwitCasting, or any creator.
